Welcome to the website of Traveling Treasure TRUNK

Wondering what Traveling Treasure TRUNK is?
You're in the right place!

TRUNK, now in its 33rd year of existence, is an undergraduate children’s theater troupe and community service organization from Tufts University in Somerville. On weekdays, we travel to schools and community centers around the Greater Boston area and perform shows for students. We write our own plays, make our own costumes, and sing our own songs, all of which amount to an interactive, fun, and engaging morning!

This semester, our play follows a fish who ventures to the deep-sea town of Bubbleburg! Our fish friend and the Bubbleburg community set out to solve the mystery of squiggles which have appeared in the town, and in the process, learn that the best way to accomplish a task is to do it together.
